Welcome to SAFDA

SAFDA is an association of farmers formed to develop and ensure sustainable black rural operations and development of rural communities.

South African Farmers Development Association (SAFDA) offers economic research and business advisory services, grower training and development, lobbying and advocacy for policies thereby ensuring the sustainability of all its members.
