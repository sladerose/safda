Funding and Enterprise

The biggest challenge that black business faces in South Africa is access to capital. The division was created as a need to overcome this challenge on behalf of our growers and others businesses within SAFDA. This is done through creating strategic partnerships and tailor making solutions for growers and SAFDA businesses alike. The division also acts as a support to other divisions within SAFDA that have projects that require assistance with capital raising.
