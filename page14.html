SAFDA Farm Management Service Company (FMSC) aims to lower the cost of production by introducing new models that enable the collective “pooling” of resources for the acquisition and application of inputs (fertilizer, chemical and fuels). SAFDA FMSC will focus on the following activities, including the provision of logistic services for cane haulage, and the bulk buying of fertilizer, diesel and chemicals for our farmers

Farmers have consistently indicated that the high input and operating costs are placing their farming businesses at risk. Industry initiatives aimed at supporting black farmers to address their challenges have also not yielded much success. Current models which were driven primarily by the milling companies have not worked, resulting in the loss of area under cane, and declining yield and decreasing revenues

FMSC also wishes to assist farmers in increasing their revenue. This will be done by introducing complimentary crops and introducing new farming models.
