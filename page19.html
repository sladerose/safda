  • Land Preparation and planting of crops - SAFDA has the capacity to assist farmers : using its own tractors, and its network for farmers. Currently assisting small scale sugar cane farmers

  • Crop diversification opportunities - Introduce other crops to our famers to ensure financial sustainability - Macadamias, piggery, cotton livestock, cash crops, - according to DARD research stations
